Theorem I.

布劳威尔不动点定理

Brouwer Fixed Point Theorem

拖动红色不动点改变其位置。箭头表示位移 f(x) − x;箭头消失之处即为不动点。
Drag the red dot. Arrows show f(x) − x; they vanish at the fixed point.

CN核心思想。非空紧致凸集上的连续自映射必有不动点——这是一个纯拓扑保证。

这里的映射是绕 $x^*$ 的旋转-收缩 $f(x) = x^* + \alpha\,R(\theta)(x - x^*)$。无论你怎么拖动 $x^*$ 或转动角度,不动点必然存在。

ENCore idea. Every continuous self-map of a non-empty compact convex set has a fixed point — a purely topological guarantee.

The map shown is a rotation-contraction $f(x) = x^* + \alpha\,R(\theta)(x - x^*)$ about a draggable centre. The fixed point persists under every continuous deformation.

定理 · Theorem

ENLet $K \subset \mathbb{R}^n$ be a non-empty, compact, convex set and let $f: K \to K$ be continuous. Then there exists $x^* \in K$ with $f(x^*) = x^*$.

CN设 $K \subset \mathbb{R}^n$ 为非空紧致凸集,$f: K \to K$ 连续。则存在 $x^* \in K$ 使 $f(x^*) = x^*$。

证明 · Proof

English Proof — via the no-retraction theorem

It suffices to prove the result for the closed unit ball $B^n = \{ x \in \mathbb{R}^n : \|x\| \le 1 \}$, since any non-empty compact convex set in $\mathbb{R}^n$ is homeomorphic to $B^k$ for some $k \le n$, and the existence of a fixed point is preserved under homeomorphism.

Suppose, for contradiction, that $f : B^n \to B^n$ is continuous and has no fixed point: $f(x) \ne x$ for all $x$. Define

$$ r(x) \;=\; x + t(x)\bigl(x - f(x)\bigr), $$

where $t(x) \ge 0$ is the unique non-negative scalar making $\|r(x)\| = 1$. Geometrically, $r(x)$ is the point at which the ray from $f(x)$ through $x$ meets the boundary $S^{n-1}$. Because $x \ne f(x)$ everywhere, $t(x)$ is well-defined and continuous, hence $r : B^n \to S^{n-1}$ is continuous. Moreover, if $x \in S^{n-1}$, the ray exits $B^n$ at $x$ itself, so $r(x) = x$.

Therefore $r$ is a continuous retraction of $B^n$ onto $S^{n-1}$. But the no-retraction theorem — a standard consequence of $\widetilde H_{n-1}(B^n) = 0$ while $\widetilde H_{n-1}(S^{n-1}) \cong \mathbb{Z}$ — forbids such a map. Contradiction. Hence some fixed point must exist. ∎

中文证明 — 基于 Sperner 引理的组合方法

不失一般性,把 $K$ 取为标准单纯形 $\Delta^n = \{ x \in \mathbb{R}^{n+1}_{\ge 0} : \sum_{i=0}^{n} x_i = 1 \}$。给定连续映射 $f: \Delta^n \to \Delta^n$,对 $\Delta^n$ 作 $k$ 重重心剖分,得到细网格 $\mathcal{T}_k$。

标号规则。对每个顶点 $v$,赋一个标号 $\ell(v) \in \{0,1,\dots,n\}$,要求

$$ \ell(v) \in \{\, i : v_i > 0 \;\text{且}\; f(v)_i \le v_i \,\}. $$

此集合非空——若不然,$f(v)_i > v_i$ 对所有 $v_i > 0$ 的指标 $i$ 成立,从而 $\sum_i f(v)_i > \sum_i v_i = 1$,与 $f(v) \in \Delta^n$ 矛盾。再要求位于 $\Delta^n$ 第 $j$ 面 $\{x_j = 0\}$ 上的顶点其标号不为 $j$,这是合法约束。

Sperner 引理。在此标号下,存在一个"完整"的小单纯形 $T_k$——其 $n+1$ 个顶点恰好取齐 $\{0,1,\dots,n\}$ 的全部标号。

取极限。设 $T_k$ 的顶点为 $v_0^{(k)},\dots,v_n^{(k)}$,标号 $i$ 在 $v_i^{(k)}$ 处取到。$k\to\infty$ 时单纯形直径趋于零,经子列设 $v_i^{(k)} \to x^*$。由标号定义

$$ f\bigl(v_i^{(k)}\bigr)_i \;\le\; \bigl(v_i^{(k)}\bigr)_i \quad \text{对每个 } i. $$

取 $k \to \infty$,由 $f$ 连续得 $f(x^*)_i \le x^*_i$ 对所有 $i$。再由 $\sum_i f(x^*)_i = \sum_i x^*_i = 1$,各分量必须取等号,故 $f(x^*) = x^*$。∎

经济学应用 · Economic Application — Walrasian Equilibrium

EN — Existence of competitive equilibrium

Consider an exchange economy with $L$ goods, prices $p \in \Delta = \{ p \in \mathbb{R}^L_+ : \sum_\ell p_\ell = 1 \}$, and aggregate excess demand $z(p)$ satisfying continuity, homogeneity, and Walras' law $p \cdot z(p) = 0$. Define the price-adjustment map

$$ T(p)_\ell \;=\; \frac{p_\ell + \max\{0,\, z_\ell(p)\}}{1 + \sum_{\ell'} \max\{0,\, z_{\ell'}(p)\}}. $$

$T : \Delta \to \Delta$ is continuous. By Brouwer's theorem there exists $p^* = T(p^*)$. Multiplying both sides by $z_\ell(p^*)$, summing over $\ell$, and applying Walras' law forces $\max\{0, z_\ell(p^*)\} = 0$ for every $\ell$ — that is, supply meets demand in every market.

Intuition. The map raises prices where there is excess demand and lowers them where there is excess supply (Walras' tâtonnement). The simplex is compact and convex; the adjustment is continuous; so Brouwer makes the equilibrium topologically inevitable. We cannot in general write $p^*$ down explicitly, but its existence is guaranteed by the geometry.

CN —— 一般均衡的存在性

设 $L$ 种商品的交换经济,价格在单纯形 $\Delta$ 上,超额需求 $z(p)$ 在标准假设下连续并满足 Walras 法则 $p \cdot z(p) = 0$。定义价格调整映射(同上),它把 $\Delta$ 连续地映到自身。由 Brouwer 定理,存在 $p^* = T(p^*)$,代数化简后即得 $z_\ell(p^*) \le 0$ 对所有 $\ell$ 成立——这正是市场出清条件。

直觉。该映射"超额需求市场涨价、超额供给市场跌价"。几何上,单纯形紧致凸,调整过程连续,Brouwer 定理告诉我们:这种"撞墙必反弹"的连续过程一定存在静止点。我们不需要解出 $p^*$,拓扑结构本身就保证了均衡的存在——这就是 Arrow–Debreu (1954) 一般均衡存在性证明的核心思想。

Theorem II.

角谷不动点定理

Kakutani Fixed Point Theorem

关闭"竖直填充"后,函数与对角线 y = x 不再相交——Brouwer 失效。
Without the vertical fill, no fixed point exists; Kakutani fixes this for set-valued maps.

CN核心思想。当一个普通函数有"跳跃"而越过对角线时,Brouwer 失效。Kakutani 允许在跳跃处填充凸值集合,只要这个集合与对角线相交,不动点就保住了。

这正是博弈论中"无差异时混合策略"的几何写照,也是 Nash 均衡存在性证明的基础。

ENCore idea. If a discontinuous function jumps over the diagonal, Brouwer fails. Kakutani lets the graph be "filled in" with a convex segment at the jump — and this segment is forced to meet the diagonal.

This is the geometry of indifference in best-response correspondences, and the basis of Nash's existence theorem.

定理 · Theorem

ENLet $K \subset \mathbb{R}^n$ be non-empty, compact, convex, and let $\varphi: K \rightrightarrows K$ be a correspondence such that (i) $\varphi(x)$ is non-empty and convex for every $x \in K$, and (ii) $\varphi$ has a closed graph: $\mathrm{Gr}(\varphi) = \{(x,y) : y \in \varphi(x)\}$ is closed in $K \times K$. Then there exists $x^* \in K$ with $x^* \in \varphi(x^*)$.

CN设 $K \subset \mathbb{R}^n$ 非空紧凸,对应 $\varphi: K \rightrightarrows K$ 满足 (i) $\varphi(x)$ 对每个 $x$ 非空且凸,(ii) $\varphi$ 的图 $\mathrm{Gr}(\varphi) = \{(x,y) : y \in \varphi(x)\}$ 在 $K \times K$ 中闭。则存在 $x^* \in K$ 使 $x^* \in \varphi(x^*)$。

证明 · Proof

English Proof — via Brouwer + simplicial approximation

For each integer $k \ge 1$, take a simplicial subdivision $\mathcal{T}_k$ of $K$ with mesh $\le 1/k$. At every vertex $v$ of $\mathcal{T}_k$, choose any $f_k(v) \in \varphi(v)$ (non-empty by (i)). Extend $f_k$ to all of $K$ by barycentric linear interpolation on each simplex: for $x = \sum_i \lambda_i v_i$ with $\lambda_i \ge 0$, $\sum_i \lambda_i = 1$, set $f_k(x) = \sum_i \lambda_i f_k(v_i)$.

Each $f_k(v) \in \varphi(v) \subset K$, and convexity of $K$ ensures $f_k(x) \in K$. Continuity is built into the construction, so $f_k: K \to K$ is continuous. By Brouwer, $f_k$ has a fixed point $x_k = f_k(x_k)$.

By compactness of $K$, pass to a subsequence so that $x_k \to x^*$. The point $x_k$ lies in some simplex of $\mathcal{T}_k$ with vertices $v_0^{(k)},\dots,v_n^{(k)}$, all within $1/k$ of $x_k$. Write

$$ x_k \;=\; \sum_{i=0}^{n} \lambda_i^{(k)}\, f_k\bigl(v_i^{(k)}\bigr), \qquad f_k\bigl(v_i^{(k)}\bigr) \in \varphi\bigl(v_i^{(k)}\bigr). $$

Pass to a further subsequence so that $\lambda_i^{(k)} \to \lambda_i^*$ and $f_k(v_i^{(k)}) \to y_i^*$. Since $v_i^{(k)} \to x^*$, $f_k(v_i^{(k)}) \to y_i^*$, and $(v_i^{(k)}, f_k(v_i^{(k)})) \in \mathrm{Gr}(\varphi)$, the closed graph hypothesis gives $y_i^* \in \varphi(x^*)$ for each $i$. Then convexity of $\varphi(x^*)$ yields

$$ x^* \;=\; \sum_i \lambda_i^*\, y_i^* \;\in\; \varphi(x^*). $$∎

中文证明 — 经 Brouwer 逼近

固定 $k \ge 1$,对 $K$ 作直径 $\le 1/k$ 的单纯形剖分 $\mathcal{T}_k$。对每个顶点 $v$,任取 $f_k(v) \in \varphi(v)$(由 (i) 非空),然后在每个小单纯形内对 $f_k$ 作重心线性延拓。由 $\varphi$ 值与 $K$ 的凸性,$f_k: K \to K$ 连续。

由 Brouwer 定理,$f_k$ 有不动点 $x_k$。$K$ 紧致,设子列 $x_k \to x^*$。$x_k$ 落在某小单纯形,其顶点 $v_0^{(k)},\dots,v_n^{(k)}$ 距 $x_k$ 不超过 $1/k$,于是

$$ x_k = \sum_{i=0}^n \lambda_i^{(k)}\, f_k\bigl(v_i^{(k)}\bigr), \quad f_k\bigl(v_i^{(k)}\bigr) \in \varphi\bigl(v_i^{(k)}\bigr). $$

再取子列使 $\lambda_i^{(k)} \to \lambda_i^*$、$f_k(v_i^{(k)}) \to y_i^*$。由 $v_i^{(k)} \to x^*$ 及图闭性,$y_i^* \in \varphi(x^*)$。再由 $\varphi(x^*)$ 凸:

$$ x^* = \sum_i \lambda_i^*\, y_i^* \in \varphi(x^*). $$∎

经济学应用 · Economic Application — Nash Equilibrium

EN — Existence of Nash equilibrium

Consider a finite normal-form game with players $i = 1,\dots,n$, finite pure-action sets $A_i$, and expected payoffs $u_i: \prod_j \Delta(A_j) \to \mathbb{R}$ where $\Delta(A_j)$ is the simplex of mixed strategies. Let $\Sigma = \prod_i \Delta(A_i)$ — compact and convex. The best-response correspondence

$$ \mathrm{BR}_i(\sigma_{-i}) \;=\; \arg\max_{\sigma_i \in \Delta(A_i)} u_i(\sigma_i, \sigma_{-i}) $$

is non-empty (continuous function on a compact set), convex (linear-in-$\sigma_i$ optimization), and has closed graph (Berge's maximum theorem). The joint correspondence $\mathrm{BR}(\sigma) = \prod_i \mathrm{BR}_i(\sigma_{-i})$ thus satisfies all of Kakutani's hypotheses on $\Sigma$. By Kakutani, there exists $\sigma^* \in \mathrm{BR}(\sigma^*)$ — that is, $\sigma_i^* \in \mathrm{BR}_i(\sigma_{-i}^*)$ for every $i$ — which is precisely the definition of a Nash equilibrium (Nash, 1950).

Intuition. When a player is indifferent between several pure strategies, every mixture among them is a best response — so the best-response "function" is genuinely set-valued. Brouwer cannot handle this. Kakutani's contribution is exactly the right generalization: as long as the set-valued map is convex-valued and has a closed graph, the fixed-point conclusion survives. This is the geometry of the vertical fill in the visualization above: indifference becomes a vertical segment, which is forced by topology to meet the diagonal of "best response equals own strategy".

CN —— Nash 均衡的存在性

有限博弈的混合策略空间 $\Sigma = \prod_i \Delta(A_i)$ 紧致凸。每个玩家的最佳反应

$$ \mathrm{BR}_i(\sigma_{-i}) \;=\; \arg\max_{\sigma_i \in \Delta(A_i)} u_i(\sigma_i,\sigma_{-i}) $$

非空(连续函数在紧集有最大值)、凸(线性优化的解集是凸的),由 Berge 极值定理具有闭图。整体最佳反应 $\mathrm{BR}: \Sigma \rightrightarrows \Sigma$ 满足 Kakutani 的全部条件,故存在 $\sigma^* \in \mathrm{BR}(\sigma^*)$,即 Nash 均衡。

直觉。当玩家对若干纯策略无差异时,任意混合都最优,最佳反应不再是函数而是一段"区间"——这正是上方可视化中那段竖直填充的经济含义。Brouwer 处理不了这种集合值跳跃,Kakutani 恰好是它的"集合值升级版"。Nash 论文里那句"this is an immediate corollary of Kakutani"就是这个意思:数学难度在 Kakutani,Nash 均衡的存在性几乎是它的一行推论。

Theorem III.

巴拿赫不动点定理

Banach Fixed Point (Contraction Mapping) Theorem

点击画布选择起点 x₀。轨迹以几何速率 L 收敛到不动点。
Click the canvas to pick x₀. The trajectory contracts toward x* at rate L.

CN核心思想。完备度量空间上的压缩映射(Lipschitz 常数 $L < 1$)有唯一不动点;任意起点的迭代序列以 $L^n$ 速率收敛。

与 Brouwer/Kakutani 仅给"存在"不同,Banach 给出了一个可计算的算法——这正是动态规划里 Bellman 算子收敛性的来源。

ENCore idea. A contraction on a complete metric space has a unique fixed point. Iterates from any starting point converge geometrically at rate $L$.

Where Brouwer and Kakutani only give existence, Banach gives a constructive algorithm: just iterate. This is exactly why the Bellman operator in dynamic programming converges to a unique value function.

定理 · Theorem

ENLet $(X, d)$ be a non-empty complete metric space and $T: X \to X$ a contraction: there exists $L \in [0, 1)$ with $d(T(x), T(y)) \le L\, d(x, y)$ for all $x, y \in X$. Then $T$ has a unique fixed point $x^* \in X$, and for every $x_0 \in X$ the iterates $x_{n+1} = T(x_n)$ satisfy $x_n \to x^*$ with the explicit error bound $d(x_n, x^*) \le \dfrac{L^n}{1-L}\, d(x_1, x_0)$.

CN设 $(X, d)$ 是非空完备度量空间,$T: X \to X$ 是压缩映射(存在 $L \in [0,1)$ 使 $d(T(x), T(y)) \le L\, d(x,y)$)。则 $T$ 有唯一不动点 $x^*$,任意起点的迭代 $x_{n+1} = T(x_n)$ 收敛到 $x^*$,且 $d(x_n, x^*) \le \dfrac{L^n}{1-L}\, d(x_1, x_0)$。

证明 · Proof

English Proof

Existence and convergence. Fix $x_0 \in X$ and define $x_n = T^n(x_0)$. By induction on the contraction inequality,

$$ d(x_{n+1}, x_n) \;\le\; L\, d(x_n, x_{n-1}) \;\le\; \cdots \;\le\; L^n\, d(x_1, x_0). $$

For any $m > n$, the triangle inequality and the geometric series give

$$ d(x_m, x_n) \;\le\; \sum_{k=n}^{m-1} d(x_{k+1}, x_k) \;\le\; d(x_1, x_0) \sum_{k=n}^{m-1} L^k \;\le\; \frac{L^n}{1-L}\, d(x_1, x_0), $$

which tends to $0$ as $n \to \infty$. So $\{x_n\}$ is Cauchy, and by completeness $x_n \to x^* \in X$. Continuity of $T$ (immediate from the Lipschitz condition) gives

$$ T(x^*) = T\bigl(\lim x_n\bigr) = \lim T(x_n) = \lim x_{n+1} = x^*. $$

Uniqueness. If $T(y^*) = y^*$ as well, then $d(x^*, y^*) = d(T(x^*), T(y^*)) \le L\, d(x^*, y^*)$, and $L < 1$ forces $d(x^*, y^*) = 0$.

Error bound. Letting $m \to \infty$ in the Cauchy estimate above (with $n$ fixed) gives the stated bound. ∎

中文证明

存在性与收敛。取 $x_0 \in X$,定义 $x_n = T^n(x_0)$。由压缩不等式迭代得 $d(x_{n+1}, x_n) \le L^n d(x_1, x_0)$。对 $m > n$,

$$ d(x_m, x_n) \;\le\; \sum_{k=n}^{m-1} d(x_{k+1}, x_k) \;\le\; \frac{L^n}{1-L}\, d(x_1, x_0) \;\xrightarrow{n\to\infty}\; 0, $$

故 $\{x_n\}$ 为 Cauchy 列,由完备性 $x_n \to x^* \in X$。$T$ Lipschitz 故连续,

$$ T(x^*) = T(\lim x_n) = \lim T(x_n) = \lim x_{n+1} = x^*. $$

唯一性。若另有不动点 $y^*$,则 $d(x^*, y^*) = d(T(x^*), T(y^*)) \le L \cdot d(x^*, y^*)$,$L < 1$ 蕴含 $d(x^*, y^*) = 0$。

误差估计。在 Cauchy 估计中令 $m \to \infty$ 即得。∎

经济学应用 · Economic Application — Bellman Equation

EN — The Bellman equation in dynamic programming

Consider an infinite-horizon discounted decision problem: state $s \in S$ (compact), action $a \in A(s)$, instantaneous reward $u(s,a)$ (continuous, bounded), transition $Q(\cdot \mid s, a)$ on $S$, discount factor $\beta \in [0, 1)$. The value function $V: S \to \mathbb{R}$ satisfies the Bellman equation

$$ V(s) \;=\; \max_{a \in A(s)} \Bigl\{\, u(s, a) + \beta \int_S V(s')\, Q(ds' \mid s, a) \,\Bigr\} \;=:\; (T V)(s). $$

Take $X = C_b(S)$, the space of bounded continuous functions on $S$ with sup-norm — a complete metric space. The Bellman operator $T$ is a contraction with modulus $\beta$:

$$ \|T V_1 - T V_2\|_\infty \;\le\; \beta\, \|V_1 - V_2\|_\infty. $$

By Banach's theorem, $T$ has a unique fixed point $V^* \in C_b(S)$ — the value function — and for any initial guess $V_0$, the value iteration $V_{n+1} = T V_n$ converges to $V^*$ at geometric rate, with $\|V_n - V^*\|_\infty \le \beta^n / (1-\beta) \cdot \|V_1 - V_0\|_\infty$.

Intuition. The discount factor $\beta < 1$ literally is the contraction modulus — tomorrow's payoff difference is worth only $\beta$ times today's. Banach's theorem then guarantees three things at once: (i) the value function exists; (ii) it is the only solution to the Bellman equation; (iii) we can compute it by iterating from any starting guess, with an a-priori geometric error bound. This is the theoretical foundation of essentially every dynamic-programming algorithm in economics, and equally of value iteration in reinforcement learning.

CN —— 动态规划中的 Bellman 方程

考虑无限期贴现决策问题:状态 $s \in S$、行动 $a \in A(s)$、单期效用 $u(s,a)$、转移核 $Q(\cdot \mid s,a)$、贴现率 $\beta \in [0,1)$。值函数 $V$ 满足 Bellman 方程

$$ V(s) \;=\; \max_{a \in A(s)} \Bigl\{\, u(s,a) + \beta \int V(s')\, Q(ds' \mid s, a) \,\Bigr\} \;=:\; (T V)(s). $$

取 $X = C_b(S)$ 配 sup 范数(完备)。直接验证可得 Bellman 算子 $T$ 是压缩常数为 $\beta$ 的压缩映射:$\|TV_1 - TV_2\|_\infty \le \beta\, \|V_1 - V_2\|_\infty$。由 Banach 定理:

(1) 值函数 $V^*$ 唯一存在; (2) 任取初值 $V_0$,值迭代 $V_{n+1} = T V_n$ 收敛至 $V^*$; (3) 误差 $\|V_n - V^*\|_\infty \le \dfrac{\beta^n}{1-\beta}\, \|V_1 - V_0\|_\infty$,几何速率收敛。

直觉。贴现率 $\beta$ 直接就是压缩常数——未来每被推迟一期就被打 $\beta$ 折,所以两个候选值函数的差距每经一次 Bellman 算子就缩小 $\beta$ 倍。Banach 定理同时给了三件事:存在性、唯一性、可证明收敛的迭代算法。这是从最优增长模型、消费–储蓄问题,到强化学习中值迭代算法的全部数学基础。贴现率本身就是收敛速率——这是动态宏观经济学最简洁的等式之一。

Continuity gives existence — contraction gives an algorithm.
